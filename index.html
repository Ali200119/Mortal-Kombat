Games

Mortal Kombat (2011)

Mortal Kombat 9 is a fighting game developed by NetherRealm Studios (formerly known as Midway Games) for PlayStation 3, Xbox 360 and PC gaming platforms. It is the ninth installment in the main Mortal Kombat series and is a replica of the franchise. The new MK is both a storyline sequel to Mortal Kombat: Armageddon and a retelling/reboot of the story of the first three games.

Mortal Kombat X

Mortal Kombat X is a multi-platform fighting game developed by NetherRealm Studios for the PlayStation 4, Xbox One and PC gaming platforms. It is the eleventh installment in the Mortal Kombat game series and a sequel to 2011's installment, Mortal Kombat 9, taking place 25 years later after the events of its predecessor.

Mortal Kombat 11

Mortal Kombat 11 is a 2019 fighting game developed by NetherRealm Studios for the PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S PC. Running on a heavily modified version of Unreal Engine 3, it is the eleventh main installment in the Mortal Kombat series and a sequel to 2015's Mortal Kombat X.
